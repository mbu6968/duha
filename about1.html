About DUHA

All praise is due to Allah, the Lord of the worlds. Peace and blessings be upon His noble Messenger, Muhammad ﷺ, his family, and his companions.

DUHA is a group of individual muslim dedicated to help brothers and sisters with an online qur'an website ,in case of emergence about reading al qur'an when it is not close or not available at the moment. The Qur'an strongly encourages us to contribute and sacrifice (whether small or big) for the sake of Allah. Allah Subhanahu Wata alla said:

Dear brothers and sisters, Allah has honored us with Islam and with the Qur'an, a light and guidance for all of humanity. To support this religion and spread the Book of Allah is one of the greatest acts of worship, for it continues to benefit people even after we return to Allah. Allah Almighty commands us:

﴿أَنفِقُوا مِمَّا رَزَقْنَاكُم مِّن قَبْلِ أَن يَأْتِيَ يَوْمٌ لَّا بَيْعٌ فِيهِ وَلَا خُلَّةٌ وَلَا شَفَاعَةٌ﴾ "Spend from what We have provided for you before there comes a Day when there will be no bargaining, no friendship, and no intercession." [Al-Baqarah 2:254] And He says: ﴿مَثَلُ الَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ أَمْوَالَهُمْ فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ كَمَثَلِ حَبَّةٍ أَنبَتَتْ سَبْعَ سَنَابِلَ فِي كُلِّ سُنبُلَةٍ مِّائَةُ حَبَّةٍ ۗ وَاللَّهُ يُضَاعِفُ لِمَن يَشَاءُ﴾ "The example of those who spend their wealth in the way of Allah is like a seed that grows seven spikes; in each spike is a hundred grains. And Allah multiplies [the reward] for whom He wills." [Al-Baqarah 2:261] The Messenger of Allah ﷺ also encouraged us not to look down on any contribution, no matter how small. He said: «اتَّقُوا النَّارَ وَلَوْ بِشِقِّ تَمْرَةٍ» "Protect yourselves from the Hellfire, even with half of a date (in charity)." [Bukhari & Muslim]

Whoever contributes to spreading the Qur'an, building a masjid, printing an Islamic book, teaching a student, or supporting the poor, Allah records for him a continuous charity (sadaqah jariyah) whose reward never ends. The Prophet ﷺ said: «مَنْ دَلَّ عَلَى خَيْرٍ فَلَهُ مِثْلُ أَجْرِ فَاعِلِهِ» "Whoever guides someone to goodness will have a reward like the one who did it." [Muslim]

Therefore, let us give from what Allah has given us, whether much or little, in order to support His religion. Let us take part in spreading the Qur'an, for every letter read by a believer because of your effort will be a light for you on the Day of Judgment.May Allah accept from us our small contributions and multiply them like mountains. May He make us among those who honor His Book and support His Deen with our wealth, our time, and our efforts.
